Explore the Nike Metcon 6





Meet the Nike Metcon 6

Our most stable training shoe is more breathable than ever. Turn it up in the Nike Metcon 6—featuring an all-mesh upper that helps you cool down faster so you can come back stronger.





Cool Down Faster

Your feet and hands cool your body temp the most. The shoe's all-mesh upper keeps air flowing through, helping you release that heat quicker during and between sets. Plus, that mesh makes the shoe more flexible—giving a lighter, softer feel for double-unders and box jumps.





Lift with Control

All that stability is back. You'll stay sturdy for lifts with the wider heel. For an even stronger base, pop in the Hyperlift heel inserts (8mm lift) and hit your max depth for squats, wall balls, snatches and cleans.





Climb with Confidence

Adding breathability didn't sacrifice durability. That mesh is reinforced to withstand rope climbs and high-intensity workouts—and it looks great outside the gym, too
